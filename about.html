About WoodLogs Preschool & Day Care

Our Story

WoodLogs Preschool & Day Care is a nurturing early learning space designed to provide holistic development for young children. We focus on play-based and structured learning approaches that help children grow emotionally, socially, physically and intellectually.

Founded by IIT & NIT alumni, WoodLogs ensures quality education, safety and care during the most important formative years of a child's life. Our mission is to create a foundation for lifelong learning while preserving the natural curiosity and creativity of every child.
